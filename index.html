Hi! I'm Blake.

I am an entrepreneur, developer, investor, speaker and animal rights advocate. I am the Co-Founder and CEO of Nectar, where we are restoring supply chains by building strong communities around great local food. My web development company (Power T Media) builds websites, apps and portals for businesses on our mission to empower people through technology. Lately, I am partiularly interested in Web3 and the potential it has to empower the individual and fundamentally change the way value is understood, exchanged and accrued online.

About Me

I believe strongly that people from different backgrounds working together on a worthy mission have the best shot at producing incredible results. To this end, I am always open to collaborate and build businesses that solve real problems in our world. I function well as a CEO, CTO or CIO and can articulate complex visions into actionable business processes. The brands I am a part of often view me as their linear problem solver. Even if we don't work together professionally, if you are trying to make a difference in the world it would be my honor to help in any way possible.
