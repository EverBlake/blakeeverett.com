About Me

I believe strongly that people from different backgrounds working together on a worthy mission have the best shot at producing incredible results. To this end, I am always open to collaborate and build businesses that solve real problems in our world. I function well as a CEO, CTO or CIO and can articulate complex visions into actionable business processes. The brands I am a part of often view me as their linear problem solver. Even if we don't work together professionally, if you are trying to make a difference in the world it would be my honor to help in any way possible.

Skills

Web Based HTML, CSS, Javascript including React (I Like Next), Solidity, Some Rust, Some Python, Some PHP, API Integrations, SQL, Worked with at least half a dozen databases, comfortable with every prototyping and no code tool such as Figma. I am comfortable using these skills where needed and develop others to creatively solve problems with an understanding of every stage of project development. Soft Skills I am comfortable working with and leading teams with differing incentives toward a common objective. I pride myself on being easy to understand and work with, but I am also clear and direct about the tasks that need to be accomplished. I have led rugby teams, adoption floors and software integrated real world businesses and my philosophy has always been the same throughout: lead by example, meaning if I am asked to lead a team I expect to be the hardest working member on it.
